My Story

Who Am I?

Hi there! I'm Susannah Jones. I'm a British American game programmer with two years of AAA industry experience and an educational background in computer science and architecture (the building/design kind, not the computer kind). I've played games and built things my whole life, and nothing brings me more joy than working on a team to bring a game project to fruition!

I'm very passionate about UI/UX design and implementation and ensuring that all players have a positive user experience. I can be reached at susannahcodes@gmail.com or on Twitter @susannahcodes.
